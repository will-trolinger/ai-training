Free AI Platforms for Advisors

Now that you understand what AI can do, here are the platforms you can use - all accessible on school Chromebooks with free educational access.

ChatGPT

100% FREE for K-12 Teachers (US) through June 2027! Most comprehensive: web search, code execution, file uploads, image generation, Advanced Voice Mode. Verify via SheerID.

Google Gemini

FREE for all Google Workspace for Education users. Integrated Google search, works with Docs/Sheets/Gmail. Automatic access if your school uses Google Workspace.

Canva for Education

100% FREE FOREVER for K-12. AI-powered design tools, millions of templates. Best for visual content: flyers, presentations, graphics. Verify with school email.

Perplexity

Free basic tier for everyone. Built specifically for web search - always cites sources. Best for research, current information, and fact-checking.

Which Platform for Which Task?

If you need to... Use this
Write or edit emails, documents, communications ChatGPT or Gemini
Research current information with sources Perplexity
Create visual materials (flyers, presentations) Canva
Practice mock interviews with voice ChatGPT (mobile app)
Analyze a document or resume ChatGPT
Work within Google Workspace Gemini
Create a timeline or event plan ChatGPT
