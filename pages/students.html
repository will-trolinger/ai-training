AI Opportunities for Students

Your students can also benefit from AI tools, especially for career preparation.

Free Access for Students

  • Google Gemini: 100% FREE with school Google accounts (all ages, with guardrails for under 18)
  • Canva for Education: 100% FREE forever for K-12 students when invited by a verified teacher
  • Microsoft Copilot: FREE for students 13+ if school has Microsoft 365 Education
  • ChatGPT: Free basic tier for everyone. Premium access through school districts that adopt ChatGPT Edu.
  • Perplexity: Free basic tier for everyone.

How Students Can Use AI

  • Resume Help: Draft and improve resume content
  • Interview Practice: Use voice mode for mock interviews at home
  • Career Research: Explore job requirements and pathways
  • Cover Letters: Get help starting and refining applications
  • Skill Building: Learn about certifications and training options

A Note on Device Access

Some schools have Chromebook carts rather than 1:1 devices, and phones are often locked up during the day. Students can:

  • Access AI tools during designated times in your office
  • Use AI at home on personal devices
  • Work with you during senior seminar or advisory periods
  • Practice mock interviews at home with voice mode
