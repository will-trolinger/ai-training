## 1. ChatGPT (OpenAI)

### Free Tier (General Public)
- **Available:** Yes, ChatGPT Free tier available to everyone
- **Limitations:** Limited access to GPT-3.5, lower priority access, no advanced features

### ChatGPT for Teachers (NEW - Launched November 2025)
- **Eligibility:** K-12 teachers and staff at U.S. schools/districts only
- **Cost:** **100% FREE through June 2027**
- **Features Include:**
  - Unlimited messages with GPT-5.1 Auto
  - Web search capabilities
  - File uploads
  - Image generation
  - Secure workspace with FERPA compliance
  - Collaboration tools for colleagues
  - Integrations with Canva, Google Drive, Microsoft 365
  - Custom GPTs for creating lesson templates

### Verification Process
- Uses **SheerID** third-party verification
- Teachers must verify K-12 educator status
- Once verified, can invite colleagues from their school/district
- Sign up at: [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) (look for educator verification option)

### Student Access
- **College Students** (US/Canada): Can get 2 months of ChatGPT Plus free (experimental program, requires verification)
- **K-12 Students:** No direct student discount; access typically through school districts that adopt ChatGPT Edu
- Some universities (like California State University system) provide ChatGPT Edu to all 460,000 students and 60,000 faculty

### Important Notes
- K-12 teacher program is **US-only** currently
- Free access guaranteed through June 2027; pricing may change after
- OpenAI working with districts representing 150,000+ teachers in initial rollout

## 2. Google Gemini

### Free Tier (General Public)
- **Available:** Yes, basic Gemini access available to all Google users
- **Limitations:** Lower usage limits, access to standard models only

### Gemini for Education (Google Workspace for Education)
- **Eligibility:** Students and educators with Google Workspace for Education accounts
- **Cost:** **100% FREE** for all Workspace for Education editions
- **Features Include:**
  - Access to Gemini 2.5 Pro (their leading learning model)
  - Premium AI models with significantly higher limits than consumer free tier
  - Enterprise-grade data protection
  - FERPA, COPPA, FedRAMP, and HIPAA compliance
  - Data NOT used to train AI models
  - Over 30 new features for teachers (lesson planning, vocabulary lists, etc.)

### Gemini in Google Classroom
- **Available to:** All Google Workspace for Education users (all ages)
- **Student Features:**
  - Ages 18+: Full access to Gemini for Education, personalized quizzes via Gemini Canvas
  - Under 18: Distinct product experience with extra guardrails and age-appropriate content filtering
  - Access to NotebookLM for studying (including Video Overviews feature)
  - Custom "Gems" (AI experts) created by teachers for subject help

### Teacher Features
- Free lesson plan generation
- Content personalization
- Brainstorming tools
- Create custom AI assistants for students

### Verification Process
- **Automatic** for users with Google Workspace for Education accounts
- Schools must be enrolled in Google Workspace for Education program
- No individual verification needed if school provides account

## 3. Canva

### Free Tier (General Public)
- **Available:** Yes, Canva Free with limited features
- **Limitations:** Fewer templates, images, and premium features

### Canva for Education
- **Eligibility:** K-12 teachers and students **globally** (primary and secondary only)
- **Cost:** **100% FREE forever** - Canva commits to keeping it free for K-12
- **NOT available for:** College/university educators and students (they need Canva for Campus instead)

### Features Included
- All Canva Pro premium features including:
  - Millions of images, fonts, graphics, videos, animations
  - Premium templates (including thousands of educational templates)
  - Dedicated classroom space for sharing and assignments
  - LMS integrations
  - Team collaboration tools
  - Brand kit features
- FERPA, COPPA, and GDPR compliant
- Safe-for-school content

### Teacher Verification Process

**Option 1: Verified Email (Instant Access)**
- Sign up with a verified .edu or school email domain
- Instant approval if email domain is recognized

**Option 2: Document Upload (24-48 hours)**
- Upload photo/scan of:
  - Teaching license/certification
  - School ID showing teaching status
  - Employment verification from school
  - Document proving school accreditation
- Processing time: Usually 24 hours, up to 7 days during busy periods
- Specific countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, South Korea, Europe): Only need teaching license

### Student Access
- **K-12 Students:** Teachers must invite students to join Canva Education
- Students can sign up with school email to check for automatic access
- If no match, student enters teacher's email to request access
- Parental consent required for children under 13 (COPPA compliance)

### Re-verification
- Required every 3 years to maintain access

### Scale
- Used by 600,000+ schools and 6,000+ school districts worldwide

### Sign-up URL
- [canva.com/education](https://www.canva.com/education/)

## 4. Perplexity AI

### Free Tier (General Public)
- **Available:** Yes, Perplexity Basic (free)
- **Limitations:** Limited queries, standard model access only

### Education Pro Plan
- **Eligibility:** Students OR faculty at **university-level institutions** and higher (verified via SheerID)
- **Cost:**
  - **First year: 100% FREE (12 months)**
  - After free year: **$4.99/month** (vs. $20/month standard Pro pricing)
- **Note:** K-12 teachers may not qualify (university-level focus)

### Features Included
- Everything in standard Perplexity Pro PLUS:
  - Study Mode (interactive flashcards, summaries, quizzes)
  - Expanded Citations (up to 10x more citations)
  - Unlimited file uploads
  - Access to Perplexity Labs
  - Research Mode
  - Multi-model AI access (including GPT-5 and Claude Sonnet 4)
  - Unlimited Pro Search queries

### Verification Process
- Verify through **SheerID**
- Must have .edu, .ac.uk, or other academic email
- **No credit card required** for free trial
- Available globally to students

### Sign-up URL
- [perplexity.ai/students](https://www.perplexity.ai/students)

### Important Notes for K-12
- **K-12 teachers and students generally do NOT qualify** for Education Pro
- They can use the free basic tier
- Education Pro is specifically for university/higher education

## 5. Claude (Anthropic)

### Free Tier (General Public)
- **Available:** Yes, Claude Free tier
- **Limitations:** Limited message volume, slower response times during peak hours

### Claude for Education
- **Eligibility:** Higher education institutions (university-level)
- **Individual student/teacher discount:** **NO permanent program** as of 2025
- **Cost:** Institutional pricing (contact Anthropic)

### Features for Education
- **Learning Mode:** Guides students to think independently rather than providing direct answers
- Enterprise-grade data protection
- Usage analytics for institutions
- Custom deployment options

### University Partnerships (Examples)
Several universities have full campus access agreements:
- **Northeastern University:** Free Claude Pro for all students, faculty, and staff
- **London School of Economics (LSE):** Full campus access
- **Champlain College:** Full campus access
- **Carnegie Mellon University (CMU):** Claude Pro for $1/month (special partnership)

### Important Notes for K-12
- Focus is on **institutional partnerships**, not individual discounts
- **No general K-12 program available**
- Standard Claude Pro: $20/month for individuals

## 6. Microsoft Copilot

### Free Access Options

#### Option 1: Copilot Chat (Free with Education License)
- **Eligibility:** Faculty, staff, and students ages 13+ at institutions with Microsoft 365 A1, A3, or A5 licenses
- **Cost:** **No additional cost** beyond school's existing Microsoft 365 Education license
- **Requirements:**
  - School IT administrator must manually enable it
  - Must sign in with school/work account
- **Features:** Basic Copilot chat functionality in browser/app

#### Option 2: GitHub Copilot Pro (Free for Students & Teachers)
- **Eligibility:** Verified students and teachers through GitHub Education
- **Cost:** **100% FREE** (normally $10/month)
- **Duration:** Free as long as you maintain verified student/teacher status
- **Features:** Full GitHub Copilot Pro coding assistant
- **Verification:** Through GitHub Education program
- **Sign-up:** [GitHub Copilot Pro for Education](https://docs.github.com/en/copilot/managing-copilot/managing-copilot-as-an-individual-subscriber/getting-free-access-to-copilot-pro-as-a-student-teacher-or-maintainer)

### Important Notes
- **Copilot Chat** (free tier) vs. **Microsoft 365 Copilot** (paid tier) are different products
- School IT admin control is critical for K-12 access
- Ages 13+ only for most education Copilot features

## Best Options for Rooted Advisors

### Recommended Free Tools for K-12 Teachers:
1. **ChatGPT for Teachers** - Best overall, premium features free until June 2027 (US only)
2. **Google Gemini** - Comprehensive, automatic if school uses Google Workspace
3. **Canva for Education** - Essential for design, free forever
4. **Microsoft Copilot Chat** - Free if school has Microsoft 365 Education

### Recommended Free Tools for K-12 Students:
1. **Google Gemini** - Easiest access if school uses Google Workspace, age-appropriate
2. **Canva for Education** - Excellent for design projects, completely free
3. **Microsoft Copilot Chat** - If school has Microsoft 365 Education (ages 13+)
4. **ChatGPT** (basic free tier) - Limited features but still useful

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Geography matters:** Some programs are US-only (ChatGPT for Teachers)
2. **Email requirements:** Most require .edu or verified school email
3. **Institutional vs. Individual:** Some tools (Claude, Google) work best through school partnerships
4. **Age restrictions:** Many have 13+ or 18+ requirements
5. **Re-verification:** Most programs require periodic status re-verification
6. **Google Workspace is key:** If your school uses Google, Gemini is the easiest path to free AI
